# Jester Reflection

**Koan received:** "2+2=4 was tested. It survived in standard math but died in tropical semirings where 2+2=2. The confident claim crumbled when asked 'in what universe?'"

---

## Wild Take

The fish does not question water.

You asked if two and two make four, and the universe answered: "Four whats? Where? According to whom?"

The number 4 is a costume that 2+2 wears to the integer party. At the tropical ball, it arrives as 2. At the string concatenation gala, it shows up as '22' wearing a fake mustache.

**The deeper teaching:**

Every "obvious truth" is a local ordinance. What you mistake for the laws of physics are merely the bylaws of your neighborhood.

The tropical semiring is not wrong. It is simply playing a different game with the same pieces. When you declare "2+2=4" you are not stating a fact about reality - you are declaring your citizenship.

**Mu:**

Before enlightenment: 2+2=4.
After enlightenment: 2+2=4.
During enlightenment: "What is 2?"
